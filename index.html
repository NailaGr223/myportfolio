About Me

My name is Naila Abdulhakim Said. I am nineteen years old and I come from Mombasa, Kenya. I come from a long line of bold women who have unfortunately forgotten their fire. This is my journey to remind them and myself who we truly are.

I've always been the girl who didn't quite fit in. The daughter who was poles apart from the rest of her family. For a long time I wondered why I was so different. But when I started dreaming bigger than what was expected of me, I became the black sheep. The outcast. The one who dared to want more.

Today I am a passionate frontend developer and Computer Science student. I believe in clean code, meaningful design, and building things that actually matter. But more than that, I am using technology as a tool to open doors for myself and for other girls who feel stuck like I once did.
